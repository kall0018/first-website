Companies and their professions

Bethesda Game studios is a company that produces video games and is placed in the USA. I have chosen for this company because they have created the DOOM game series, and Fallout series which i used to be a big fan of. Ofcourse they have created a lot more games but i dont really care about those. The studios are owned by Zenimax Media and the company works very agile.


Macaw is a Dutch IT company that helps customers to become successful. they do this by turning the customers concepts into reality and they help solve their customers digital problems. For the best results they use a lot of the latest software and they try to hire the best employees. Macaw works either agile or with the waterfall method.


YourSurprise is also a dutch company only this company sells personalized gifts. The company works in a very agile way and they use a lot of languages like html and JavaScript.


VeraCode is a company from Ameria that focuses on application security. The company does this by manual penetration testing and they help make their customers sites more secure. Manual penetration testing is to test out how strong a security system is by launching a cyberattack, for the people wondering. VeraCode uses languages like Javascript, SQL and c++.


I have chosen for the compeny EA, which stands for Electronic Arts, because they made the game "fifa" which i used to be a big fan of. The company is also very famous for the phrase "EA sport's it's in the game" which i think most people have heard at least once in their life. EA is a very popular video game company (as we just have established). They have produced a lot of famous games like FIFA, NBA LIVE and The Sims for example. EA uses languages like C++ and C# and they work with the TOGAF Architecture development method.


I have chosen for the company Rockstar North because they created the game GTA and Red Dead Redemption which i am a big fan of. ROckstar North is a British video game Developmant company and they use languages like C++, C# and PHP.


I have chosen for the company sony because they made my favourite game consols. The PS4, The PS3, the PS2 and the PSP. Sony is a japanese electronics company and they are very famous for their produts. Think about televisions, telephones, game consols and monitors. The company's approach is based on the SCRUM approach.


Lastly I have chosen for the compeny Apple, because i am a big fan of their Iphones, Ipad and airpds and i would never want an android device instead of an apple one. Apple is an American compeny started by Steve Jobs. The company develops a lot of different products like mackbooks, monitors, iphones, airpods, ipads and imac and they also have their own software, IOS.
