How can I scan a document and send it to my laptop at HZ?

If you want to scan a domument at HZ you must first log in with your school ID on a printer. After you have loged in succesfully you are going to have to select the option 'scan' and then place your document on the glas part of the printer. After you have done all the previous step you are going to have to press 'scan and sen' then you need to pred 'yes' and then 'yes'. If you have completed all the previous steps correctly then 'start sending' will pop up on the printer screen and you will need to press on it. At last you will need to press 'stop' and then you must log out. Good luck.

How can I print a document from my laptopt at HZ?

If you want to print a document at HZ, then you must first send the document you wish to print to the printer in A3 or A4 format. After you have completed the first step you must go to the printer and register with your school Id there. And last when you have completed the previous steps you should see your document on the printer and then you can start printing. Note that after 24 hours your document disappears from the printer.

How can i book a project space in one of the wings?

If you want to book a project space in one of the wing you you need to go to 'selfservice portal' then to 'new reservation' and then to 'book project group rooms' on the HZ website. After you have done the previous steps you will be able to choose a space you want to book. After choosing a place you will need to fill out some details and that's it. Good luck with your project.

If you want to park you can do this at Poelendaelsingel 10 by using your HZ ID. There are also some chargers at the parking space for the people that drive electric cars and there are also two parking spaces right in front of the HZ building for the disabled people
